Sudan, with its vast landscapes and rich cultural heritage, is a land of captivating beauty. From the golden sands of the Sahara Desert to the lush greenery along the Nile River, Sudan offers a diverse and mesmerizing natural scenery. The country is blessed with breathtaking landscapes, including towering mountains, expansive savannahs, and pristine coral reefs along its coastline. Unfortionatly Since the 15th of April, Sudan has experienced a period of sorrow and unrest that has left its mark on the nation. The once vibrant and hopeful atmosphere has been overshadowed by a sense of despair and disappointment. The events that unfolded have had a profound impact on the lives of Sudanese people, leaving behind a trail of pain and anguish.
